Welcome to Annapurna's doorstep

Mountain View Homestay offers an unforgettable experience in the heart of Pokhara. Wake up to stunning sunrise over the Annapurna range and enjoy authentic Nepali hospitality. Our family has welcomed travelers for over a decade, sharing home-cooked dal bhat, cultural stories, and serene mountain vibes.

Each room is equipped with comfortable bedding, hot showers, and private balconies overlooking the Himalayas. Traditional decor meets modern comfort. Guests also love our rooftop meditation deck and free cultural performances in the evening.
