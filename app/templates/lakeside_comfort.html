Your Home Away from Home by Fewa Lake

Lakeside Comfort Inn offers the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and stunning natural beauty. Located just a 3-minute walk from the serene Fewa Lake, our guesthouse provides easy access to Pokhara's best restaurants, shops, and adventure activity centers while maintaining a peaceful, relaxing atmosphere.

Our rooms are designed for comfort with plush bedding, modern bathrooms with hot water, and private balconies offering garden or mountain views. Start your day with a complimentary breakfast on our rooftop terrace overlooking the Annapurna range. We offer free WiFi, parking, and a fully equipped shared kitchen. Our friendly staff can arrange paragliding, zip-lining, boat rides, and trekking permits for the Annapurna Circuit.
