Our Story

Born in the
Heart of Himalayas

Founded in Kathmandu in 2024, Bookmandu was born from a simple idea: the best way to experience Nepal isn't from a hotel lobby — it's from a family kitchen in Pokhara, a farmhouse in Chitwan, or a teahouse on the trail to Annapurna.

We built Bookmandu to help travelers find those authentic moments, and to help Nepali families share their homes, culture, and hospitality with the world. Today, we're proud to be Nepal's fastest-growing homestay platform.

"Every booking on Bookmandu is a story waiting to happen — a connection between a traveler and a host that goes beyond commerce."

🙏
Rijwan Miya Founder & CEO
