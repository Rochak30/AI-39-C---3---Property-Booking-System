🏠 About Pahuna

Connecting Travelers with
Nepal's Heart & Homes

Pahuna is Nepal's homegrown homestay platform — connecting travelers with authentic local hosts across the Himalayas, ancient cities, and jungle lowlands. We're on a mission to make travel in Nepal more personal, more sustainable, and more rewarding — for guests and hosts alike.

✦ ✦ ✦
📖 Our Story

Born from a
Simple Idea

Founded in Kathmandu in 2026, Pahuna was born from a simple idea: the best way to experience Nepal isn't from a hotel lobby — it's from a family kitchen in Pokhara, a farmhouse in Chitwan, or a teahouse on the trail to Annapurna.

Our traveling adventures take you to breathtaking destinations, creating unforgettable memories and experiences that last a lifetime. We built Pahuna to help travelers find those authentic moments, and to help Nepali families share their homes and culture with the world.
