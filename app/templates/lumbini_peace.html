Find Inner Peace at the Birthplace of Buddha

Lumbini Peace Lodge offers a tranquil retreat just steps away from the sacred Mayadevi Temple, the birthplace of Lord Buddha. Our lodge provides clean, comfortable rooms with a peaceful garden setting, perfect for pilgrims, meditators, and travelers seeking spiritual solace.

We are located within walking distance of the Lumbini Sacred Garden, where you can explore ancient ruins, monasteries from Buddhist nations worldwide, and the peaceful Pushkarini Sacred Pond. Our staff can arrange meditation sessions, monastery visits, and guided tours of this UNESCO World Heritage Site. Enjoy simple, wholesome vegetarian meals in our garden restaurant.
