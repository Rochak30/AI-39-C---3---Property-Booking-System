Experience the Forbidden Kingdom's Rich Heritage

Welcome to Mustang Desert Homestay, nestled in the ancient walled city of Lo-Manthang, the capital of the former Kingdom of Lo. This remote region, often called "Little Tibet," offers a unique blend of Tibetan Buddhist culture, dramatic desert landscapes, and breathtaking Himalayan views of Nilgiri, Annapurna, and Dhaulagiri ranges.

Our family homestay provides an authentic experience in a traditional Mustangi home with mud-brick walls, wooden beams, and cozy common areas warmed by a traditional bukhari stove. We serve hearty Tibetan-influenced meals, including thenthuk (noodle soup), momos, and butter tea. From our rooftop, you can watch the spectacular sunrise painting the Himalayas gold and explore the 15th-century royal palace, ancient monasteries, and vibrant prayer flags that make Lo-Manthang magical.
