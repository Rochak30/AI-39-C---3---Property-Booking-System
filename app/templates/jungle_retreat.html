Immerse Yourself in the Wild Heart of Nepal

Jungle Retreat Chitwan offers an unparalleled wildlife experience on the edge of Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Our eco-friendly resort features private cottages surrounded by lush jungle, where you can wake up to the calls of exotic birds and spot rhinos from your porch.

We offer guided jungle safaris, elephant bathing experiences, canoe rides along the Rapti River, and authentic Tharu village tours. Our expert naturalists will help you spot Bengal tigers, one-horned rhinos, crocodiles, and over 500 species of birds. After a day of adventure, relax at our riverside restaurant with traditional Tharu cuisine and enjoy cultural dance performances under the stars.
