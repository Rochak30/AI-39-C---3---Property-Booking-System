Experience Old Kathmandu with Modern Comfort

Thamel Heritage House blends traditional Newari architecture with contemporary amenities. Located in the vibrant tourist hub of Thamel, our guesthouse offers a peaceful courtyard escape from the bustling streets. Each room features handcrafted woodwork, antique furniture, and modern en-suite bathrooms.

Our rooftop restaurant serves authentic Nepali cuisine with views of the Kathmandu Valley. We're steps away from Garden of Dreams, local eateries, trekking shops, and UNESCO World Heritage sites. Free parking, fully equipped kitchen access, and 24-hour front desk make your stay effortless.
